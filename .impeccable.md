## Design Context

### Users
The application is used by approximately 10 internal team members at a manufacturing/fulfillment company. The primary context of use is on mobile devices (phones) while moving around the warehouse or office. The job to be done is quickly checking the realtime status of all active orders, understanding the pipeline at a glance, and updating order statuses or adding new orders on the fly.

### Brand Personality
**Reliable, Focused, Efficient.** 
The interface should evoke confidence and speed. It is an internal operational tool, so it must prioritize clarity over decoration, but still feel like a premium, thoughtfully-built piece of software.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Bold & Structured.**
A confident, utilitarian aesthetic that uses a structured grid, clear container boundaries, and strong typography to present data. Statuses should be instantly recognizable via distinct colors and large typography. Contrast should be high to support quick reading in varying lighting conditions. Dark mode must be fully supported and functional. 

### Design Principles
1. **Information Density with Clarity:** Optimize for mobile by keeping lists compact, but use bold typographic hierarchy so nothing feels cramped. Tap targets must remain large (44px minimum).
2. **Deterministic & Meaningful Color:** Colors should exclusively communicate state or category. Use vibrant, distinct colors (via OKLCH) for statuses and categories, and desaturated, tinted neutrals for backgrounds and borders.
3. **No Decorative Noise:** Banish generic AI aesthetics (glassmorphism, gradient text, excessive shadows). Use flat cards with subtle, crisp borders and high-contrast text.
4. **Action-Oriented Realtime:** The interface must feel alive and immediate. Actions (like the FAB) should be prominent and always accessible. Data updates should feel seamless without jarring layout shifts.
